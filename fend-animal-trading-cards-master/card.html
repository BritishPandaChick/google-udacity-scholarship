Giant Panda

Pandas eat an average amount of 20 to 30 pounds of bamboo shoots a day.

  • Scientific Name: Ailuropoda melanoleuca
  • Average Length:4-6 feet (1.2-1.9 m)
  • Average Lifespan: 20 years (wild), 30 years (captivity)
  • Habitat: Mountain ranges, lowland areas especially bamboo forests

Giant pandas have the typical bear shape. They have black fur on their ears, eye patches, muzzle, legs, arms, and hsoulders. The rest of their coat is white. Scientists speculate this provides camouflage in their surroundings or help them associate themselves with other bears.
